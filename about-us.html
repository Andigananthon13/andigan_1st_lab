About us!!!

Rain&Angel Beach Resort

Welcome to Rain and Angel beach Resort, where luxury meets tranquility amidst breathtaking natural beauty. Our resort and hotel offers an unparalleled escape for discerning travelers seeking ultimate relaxation and rejuvenation.

At Rain and Angel beach Resort, we pride ourselves on providing exceptional hospitality, ensuring every guest receives personalized attention and experiences that exceed expectations. Whether you're here for a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a corporate retreat, our dedicated team is committed to curating unforgettable moments tailored to your preferences.

Indulge in our luxurious accommodations, ranging from elegant rooms to expansive suites, each meticulously designed to offer the perfect blend of comfort and sophistication. Wake up to stunning views of clean water from the beach, and unwind in style with our world-class amenities, including live band at night, photo booths, etc.
